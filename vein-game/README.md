## üéÆ About VEIN Game

**VEIN** is a hardcore survival sandbox game that combines scavenging, base building, vehicle mechanics, and dynamic world systems. Set in a post-apocalyptic Champlain Valley, players must scavenge for resources, build fortified bases, repair vehicles, craft weapons and armor, and survive against hordes of zombies.

### Key Features

- **Open-World Survival**: Explore a vast, interactive world where you can open anything, move anything, and weaponize junk
- **Base Building**: Construct fortified bases with power grids, water systems, and defensive structures
- **Vehicle System**: Hotwire cars, repair engines, change tires, and use vehicles as mobile bases
- **Deep Crafting**: Advanced crafting system requiring Industrial Welders, Hard Leather Strips, Metal Sheets, and smelting ores into ingots
- **Skill Progression**: Character development with skills, perks, and leveling systems up to level 50+
- **Dynamic World**: Realistic survival mechanics including hunger, thirst, illness, and environmental hazards
- **Multiplayer Ready**: Planned multiplayer support for cooperative and competitive gameplay

### Game Information

- **Developer**: VEIN Development Team
- **Release Date**: Early Access - October 24, 2025
- **Platform**: PC (Steam)
- **Genre**: Survival, Sandbox, Open-World
- **Game Modes**: Single-player, Multiplayer (planned)

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### What is VEIN?

VEIN is a post-apocalyptic survival sandbox game where players scavenge for resources, build bases, repair vehicles, and survive against zombies in Champlain Valley. It combines realistic survival mechanics with deep crafting and base-building systems.

### What platforms is VEIN available on?

VEIN is currently available on PC via Steam Early Access, launching October 24, 2025.

### What makes VEIN different from other survival games?

VEIN features unprecedented world interactivity‚Äîyou can open anything, move anything, and weaponize junk. The game combines elements from games like 7 Days to Die and Project Zomboid in a first-person perspective, with a focus on realistic survival mechanics and deep base-building systems.

### Is multiplayer available?

Multiplayer is planned for future updates. The game currently supports single-player gameplay, with cooperative and competitive multiplayer modes in development.

### What languages are supported?

VEIN currently supports English interface and subtitles. Additional language support may be added in future updates.

### Is there a demo available?

Yes, a free demo is available on Steam that lets you experience core gameplay, explore a small region, and progress skills up to level 50.

### What content warnings apply?

VEIN contains violence, gore, and mature themes typical of zombie survival games. It is rated for mature audiences.

### How often is the game updated?

The development team provides regular updates based on player feedback. Check the Wiki's Update History section for the latest patch notes.
